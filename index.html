About Heaven

Heaven is the highest, most blessed realm where you dwell in the presence of God. You'll be with the angels and the righteous. When entering heaven, you will have eternal joy and everlasting peace. In heaven, there will be rejoicing, praising, ane worshiping. Weather it be with your voice, your instrument, your art, your dance, or just yourself. When entering heaven, you are promised a manchine. Isn't that inticing!? I can assure you that this is the best place not just to visit, but to live!!!

How to get to heaven

Many make the mistake in thinking that in order to make it to heaven, you have to die. While that may be true, there's more to it. In order to make it into heaven, you have to live.
